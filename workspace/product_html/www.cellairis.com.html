I have been looking for "the best" case for my iPhone. I have found quite a few - many with features I liked and I really didn't care for. When I was out and about one day, I saw a Cellairis shop in a local mall, so I started looking at the many different cases. I was shown this particular case and I was hesitant at first. This one was a two-piece snap case and I have never liked any of them because the front piece was pretty easy to come off. Well, my faith has been restored in the two-piece snap cases! The Cellairis Rapture Elite Case is incredible! This has everything I have ever wanted in a case – solid body protection, an overlay on the front edge so the screen does not touch the surface and the overall feel of it is a strong, solid feel in your hand. And the feature I like the best – it takes a crowbar to take this son-of-a-gun apart, so there is no worry about the case coming apart. I got the black with gray inner silicone skin. I liked mine so much, I got a white one with black inner silicone skin for my wife’s iPhone. I think I can pretty much say, with confidence, that my search for “the perfect” iPhone case is over!
