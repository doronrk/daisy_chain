Since 1937, the name Ray-Ban has been associated with some of the most iconic styles in the history of sunglasses. From the style worn by pilots during World War II (the Aviator) to distinctive black frames favored by movie stars and musicians (the Wayfarer), Ray-Ban is behind many enduring looks of modern pop culture. High on style, Ray-Ban sunglasses are also lauded for impressive quality and sun protection.

Ray-Ban RB3025 Aviator sunglasses encompass the shape that started it all. The Ray-Ban Aviator is the brand staple originally designed for the U.S. military fighter pilots in 1937. Ray-Ban Aviator sunglasses have a timeless look with the unmistakable teardrop shaped lenses. This style allowed the Aviator to quickly spread beyond its utility, becoming popular among celebrities, rock stars, and citizens of the world alike. This is an iconic look that has endured for nearly a century. Ray-Ban RB3025 Aviator sunglasses are available with polarized lenses and are prescription friendly.
