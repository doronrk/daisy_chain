Leo Bridal Set 1 1/2 ct tw Diamonds 14K Two-Tone Gold

This ravishing bridal set for her features an engagement ring with a trio of striking Leo Diamonds framed in 14K rose gold. More round Leo Diamonds shimmer along the 14K white gold band of the engagement ring as well as the matching wedding band. The bridal set has a total diamond weight of 1 1/2 carats. The Leo Diamond is independently certified and laser-inscribed with a unique Gemscribe serial number. Diamond Total Carat Weight may range from 1.45 - 1.57 carats.

Estimated Arrival Date

The Estimated Arrival Date for our merchandise is displayed on product pages, in your shopping bag, and throughout the checkout process. We provide the Estimated Arrival as a guide to when your kay.com order should arrive. Kay Jewelers wants to be as accurate as possible when determining when you will receive your merchandise but we cannot factor certain events into the Estimated Arrival Date.

The Estimated Arrival Date DOES NOT take the following into account:

Delays in credit approval
Weather delays
Other shipping/vendor service disruptions
APO/FPO orders going through the Military Postal Service Agency (MPSA)

The Estimated Arrival Date DOES take the following into account:

Additional time needed for ring sizing
The time needed to create your personalized and special ordered items
Items shipping from different locations on different days*
Shipping method selected in checkout**

*Special orders, personalized merchandise, and Design-A-Ring orders may ship from different locations on different days, depending on the item. The Estimated Arrival Date for each item will appear in the Shopping Bag, throughout the Checkout process, and in the Order Confirmation.

**The Estimated Arrival Date on individual product pages will default to ground shipping. Selecting alternate shipping methods may change the Estimated Arrival Date.
