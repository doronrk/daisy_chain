DELIVERY
The following processing and delivery costs are for merchandise pictured and described on louisvuitton.com; Costs are calculated according to the total cost of the merchandise in your order. All prices reflect U.S. currency.

Shipping fees:
For all orders under $3,000, standard shipping is complimentary, express shipping is $25, priority is $35. For all orders $3,000 and above, all shipping options are complimentary.

Mon Monogram and Mon Damier Graphite products are delivered with complimentary shipping. Please allow up to 8 weeks for delivery. For made to order items, please allow up to 3 weeks for delivery.

Delivery & Charges:
Delivery times are not guaranteed. We ship only to a valid address in the 50 United States.

Signature Required:
Due to the high value of our products there will be a signature required on all shipments above $50 regardless of service type.

Note:
Orders may take from 24 to 36 hours to process. Once your order is processed and shipped you will receive an email notification. All delivery standards begin when you receive this email.

Standard Delivery:
Unless an expedited service is requested, your order should arrive in up to 5 business days from the time you receive the shipping confirmation email. Orders will be shipped using FedEx or UPS Home Delivery service. If an order is placed after 3:00pm (EST) on Friday or over the weekend, the earliest it will ship is Monday. For Mon Monogram and Mon Damier Graphite orders, please allow up to 8 weeks for delivery. For made to order items, please allow up to 3 weeks for delivery.

Express Delivery:
If express delivery is requested, your order will be prioritized and arrive in 2 business days (Monday - Friday) from the time you receive the shipping confirmation email. Express orders placed by 3pm (EST) Monday - Saturday will be shipped the same day you place your order, unless additional validation is required. Express orders placed after 3:00pm (EST) Saturday or anytime Sunday may not be processed until Monday. Orders will be shipped using FedEx 2 day express or UPS 2nd day air. Please note that some zip codes are not available for this service. *Not available for Mon Monogram and Mon Damier Graphite.

Overnight Delivery:
If overnight delivery is requested, your order will be prioritized and will arrive the next business day (Monday - Friday) from the time you receive the shipping confirmation email. Overnight orders placed by 3:00pm (EST) Monday - Saturday will be shipped the same day you place your order, unless additional validation is required. Overnight orders placed after 3:00 pm (EST) Saturday or anytime Sunday may not be processed until Monday. Orders will be shipped using FedEx overnight or UPS next day air. Please note that some zip codes are not available for this service. *Not available for Mon Monogram and Mon Damier Graphite.

Alaska and Hawaii Delivery:
For expedited delivery we recommend you select Express or Overnight Shipping. If you select ground shipping, our order should arrive in approximately 7 to 10 business days from time you receive the shipping confirmation email, however remote addresses may require additional delivery time.

Delivery:
Louis Vuitton only offers delivery in the country where the order has been placed online or by phone. Louis Vuitton does not deliver to APO, FPO, DPO or PO boxes.
PAYMENT
Payments are accepted via credit card, debit card or PayPal with a valid United States billing and shipping address. Accepted credit cards are Visa, American Express, Mastercard, and Discover.

When placing an order, your billing address must correspond to the address of your credit card, or we will not be able to process your order.

All transactions are secured. The Louis Vuitton website is provided with an SSL encryption system to protect personal and payment data.
RETURNS & EXCHANGES

Merchandise may be exchanged or returned for a store credit within one (1) month of the original purchase date, or may be returned for a full refund (except personalized items, Mon Monogram, Mon Damier Graphite, Made-to-Order items, watches and jewelry, and PayPal purchases) within fourteen (14) days of the original purchase date when accompanied by the original sales receipt. PayPal purchases may be exchanged or returned for store credit within one (1) month of the original purchase date, or may be returned exclusively to our online store for a full refund within fourteen (14) days of the original purchase date when accompanied by the original sales receipt. All merchandise returned for exchange, store credit or refund must be in perfect saleable condition.

Watches and jewelry may be returned at watch/jewelry boutiques for exchange in the same product category or for store credit only, no refund will be given. When returning watches or jewelry, the customer must bring back the complete product, including the packaging, instructions leaflets (with the warranty certificate on the final page), product certificates (such as COSC and GIA, where applicable) and the invoice.

A refund may only be made in the same currency country as the original purchase.

To return or exchange a Louis Vuitton product, please follow the instructions below:
Contact Client Services at +1.866.VUITTON (+1.866.884.8866) or on Twitter @LVServices for assistance and to receive a return authorization number. The product should be returned in its original packaging and condition. Our Client Services specialists will offer you the following shipping options:
- Receive via email a FedEx shipping label to facilitate the complimentary return of your product.
- Please complete the Return Voucher you received in your order and kindly include it with the return, along with the original or gift receipt.
- Remove any outer labels prior to affixing the FedEx return label and securely seal your package before dropping it off at any FedEx store or FedEx pick-up location.
- You may ship your return or exchange via any other shipping method of your choice to the address indicated on the return voucher. However, should you choose not to use the label provided to you and ship via a different carrier, Louis Vuitton cannot be held responsible for packages lost in transit, and shipping costs will not be reimbursed.
- You may also return or exchange your purchase at any Louis Vuitton store on the condition that it offers the same product category (with the exception of PayPal purchases which can only be returned or exchanged for store credit). It is not necessary to call to get an authorization number in this case, but please do not hesitate to contact us if you need any assistance.
- You will be reimbursed for the cost of the product via your original payment method.
