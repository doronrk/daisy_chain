    Feels true to size

    Overall I like this suit because it's the best maternity suit that doesn't feel like it's compressing my belly and there is room to grow. It's not your traditional tank suit that us lap swimmers prefer but it is functional for laps. Unfortunately, because it's made out of lycra and not 100% polyester it does wear out rather quickly. I've been swimming laps in it for about 60 min 3x/week for the last 3 months or so and it's getting that bagginess that you see when suits wear out. The boob area is nice because it actually has light padding in it, however it's not super tight so kind of sags when wet or baggy. Not awful and I'm still swimming laps in it comfortably at 35 wks, but could be better if made out of 100% poly and tighter in the top or more tank suit-like. Oh and that annoying clasp in the back that someone else commented on is impossible to do on your own, however I am able to get in the suit with it already clasped so it's not too big of a deal.

PregO Maternity Wear, commonly called PregO, is the only swimwear company that designs exclusively for pregnant moms. Established in 1992, PregO is known for it's high quality, excellent fit and supportive styles.

PregO, is designed with the current trends in mind and incorporates stylish silhouettes and beautiful designer prints to enhance the beauty of pregnancy. Selections include solids and prints in a variety of body styles that include classics and innovative styles for that new baby bump. Choose from tankinis, bikinis, tanks and skirts. PregO can cover all body sizes from XS to 3X.

Designed and manufactured in California, PregO is sold throughout the U.S., Canada, Asia, Australia and Europe.
