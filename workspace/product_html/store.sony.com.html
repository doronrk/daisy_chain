The heart of your system.

Bring the excitement home with 5.2 channels, 725 watts8 and iPhone® and iPod® connectivity. It also handles all of your components with 4 HDMI® inputs and 4K resolution pass-through4. Now, all of your music and movies will get the multi-layered sound quality they deserve with HD Digital Cinema Sound and auto calibration for easy set-up.

Sweet Sound

The STR-DH550 packs the best Sony®Music and Sony®Pictures audio technologies in addition to being powerful enough to deliver impressive multi-channel sound performance (145W x 7 @ 6 ohms, 1 kHz, 0.9% THD). It even supports High-Resolution file playback (up to 48k/16-bit PCM) using the convenient, front-mounted USB connector1.

Well Connected

Enjoy versatile HD connectivity with four HDMI® inputs and connect a Sony® PlayStation® entertainment system, Blu-ray Disc™ player, or any other HDMI®-capable devices2. Via MHL (Mobile High-Definition Link), easily connect a smartphone and enjoy video clips, music, and more on the big screen complete with big sound3. A front-mounted USB input even allows for easy connectivity and charging of compatible devices1.

Life less complicated.

Don’t turn on your entire home theater system just to catch the nightly news or watch a Blu-ray™ movie. HDMI® pass-through functionality allows all connected HDMI® devices, including native 4K video content, Blu-ray and Blu-ray 3D™ movies, video games and other content to conveniently pass-through to your compatible television or projector3.

Easy Setup and Use.

Optimizing home theater settings for your space using Digital Cinema Auto Calibration (D.C.A.C.) is a snap. Connect your gear, plug-in the supplied calibration microphone, and use the simplified remote to follow the on-screen prompts. Within a few short moments your receiver will be ready for action and all you have to do is sit back, relax, and enjoy.
