Description

A chic, lightweight design and glamorous detailing blend to create this essential shift dress. Finished with a perfectly placed cutout, it evokes just the right amount of elegance and allure.

  • Woven shift dress. Rounded neckline. Long sleeves with triple barrel cuffs.
  • Beaded embellishment at neckline and sleeve cuffs. V-shaped cutout at bust.
  • Back zipper closure. Lined.
  • Measures approximately 32" from shoulder to hem
  • Shell and lining: 100% Polyester
  • Hand wash
  • Measurement taken from a size small
  • Imported
