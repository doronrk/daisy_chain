Boys' ZEVO3 Comic Smash Backpack Bags

Super heroic style and fun lights come in the SKECHERS Comic Smash Backpack. Durable fabric with all over fun comic effect print in a school backpack with light-up detail and durable straps.

Construction:

  • Soft durable poly fabric
  • Two strap school backpack design
  • Measures 15 inches high by 12 inches wide by 5 inches deep
  • Contrast stitching detail
  • Solid colored web fabric stripe and bottom trim
  • All over colorful Zevo 3 superhero themed comic effect print
  • Bold lightning bolt applique detail
  • Bolt detail with colorful lights that blink when moved!
  • Front accessory zippered pocket with Zevo-3 zipper pull
  • Double zippers open generous main compartment
  • Fabric lined large main compartment
  • Zevo-3 logo ID tag
  • Dual shoulder straps with comic print detail
  • Adjustable slide buckles for cinchable fit
  • Padded mesh back panels for comfort
