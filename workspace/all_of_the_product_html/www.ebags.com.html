Detailed Product Description

Carry a few essentials around town inside this mini backpack from JanSport. The JanSport Half Pint backpack is made from polyester fabric and features a classic shape in an extra small design that makes it perfect for children and teens. This backpack has a small main compartment that can be used to hold a wallet, keys, portable electronic gadgets, or other must haves and includes a separate zip front pocket to hold your cell phone, pens, or other small items that you need to keep within easy reach. Adjustable web shoulder straps make this mini backpack easy and comfortable to carry.

Size Guide: School Backpacks

We'll help you choose the right school pack, based on your everyday campus needs. Our size ranges are based on the L x W x H of the item.

  • X-Small

    Sized to be a kid's first backpack. Holds snacks, toys and anything they can't live without.

  • Small

    Sized for students in grade school.  Holds folders, note pads, pencils, lunch.

  • Medium

    Sized for later grade & middle school students. Holds books, binders, pens, pencils, light jacket, lunch.

  • Large

    Sized for middle/high school & college students. Holds many books, folders, binders, light jacket, lunch.

  • X-Large

    Sized for students that carry it all. Holds books, binders, folders, and after-school gear.
