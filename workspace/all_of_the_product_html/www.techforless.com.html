StarTech BRACKET25SAT 2.5-inch SATA Hard Drive to 3.5-inch Drive Bay Mounting Kit

The Serial ATA hard drive mounting kit allows you to install a 2.5-inch SATA hard drive in a 3.5-inch drive bay. The 2.5 to 3.5 inches SATA hard drive mounting kit features a sturdy aluminum bracket, as well as a combined 7+15 pin SATA + LP4 Power to SATA cable - which helps simplify installation and reduce clutter within the computer case.
