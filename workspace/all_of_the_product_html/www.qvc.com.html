Your little girl will fall in love with Disney's singing Elsa doll. Wearing her beautiful turquoise snow queen dress, along with a matching tiara and shoes, Elsa sings "Let it Go" when her arm is raised and says 15 different phrases when her necklace is pressed. And to top it all off, her dress magically illuminates with blue LED lights as she sings and talks! From Disney.

  • Includes Elsa doll, tiara, shoes, and Olaf doll
  • Dress illuminated by blue LEDs
  • Sings "Let it Go" when arm is raised
  • Says 15 phrases from the movie when necklace is pressed
  • English/Spanish language options
  • Ages 3 and up
  • Requires 3 AAA batteries, not included
  • Measures 14-1/2"H
  • 90-day Limited Manufacturer's Warranty
  • Imported
