Product Overview

This 4 piece combo kit features a 3/8" cordless drill, 5 1/2" cordless circular saw, flashlight, radio console, (1) 18V Ni-Cd battery, and 18V charger. This combo kit is the perfect product addition for any ONE+™ enthusiast.
