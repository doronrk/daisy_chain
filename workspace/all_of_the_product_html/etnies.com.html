Product Overview

The Marana E-Lite is a follow-up to the instant classic Marana. It features an indestructible TPR toe cap, plus an STI Foam Lite 1 insole and an Evolution Foam midsole that wraps around the heel for maximum stability and support while providing excellent impact absorption. The wide elastic tongue straps provide a superior fit while the mesh tongue allows breathability. The grippy outsole continues the tread pattern design of the Marana that makes it the perfect skate shoe.Collaboration w/ Plan B Skateboards.
