      Classic Quatre 18k Gold Large Diamond Band Ring, Size 7

      A glittering tribute to Paris, the City of Light: The Boucheron Large Diamond Classic Quatre band ring expresses unexcelled craftsmanship with four shades of gold in four patterns.
      Details

      • From the Quatre Collection.
      • 18-karat pink, white, yellow, and Boucheron chocolate gold in smooth, ridged, and studded bands.
      • White pave diamonds set in white gold; 0.52 total diamond carat weight.
      • US ring size 7.

      Designer

      About Boucheron Paris:
      The House of Boucheron is a French family firm founded by Frederic Boucheron in 1858. Known worldwide for its exceptional pieces of high-quality jewelry, Boucheron Paris also has become renowned for luxurious perfumes and watches. Boucheron Paris enthusiasts include royal patrons and celebrities alike.
