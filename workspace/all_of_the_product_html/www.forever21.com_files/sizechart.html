How To Measure Your Inseam

How To Determine Your Bra Size

Band Size
-Measure around your ribcage, directly under the bust.
-Add 5 to this measurement to obtain your band size (i.e.: If your ribcage measurement is 29" + (5) = 34 band size.)

Cup Size
-Measure completely around the bust at the fullest point.
-Each inch of difference between this measurement and your band size is your cupsize. (i.e.: If your band size is 34" and your bust measurement is 35", you wear an 'A' cup.)

If the difference is:
1 inch = A cup    2 inches = B cup    3 inches = C cup   4 inches = D cup
