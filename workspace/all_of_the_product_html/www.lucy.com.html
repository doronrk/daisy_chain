Product Details

For fans of the Hatha, the Striped Hatha Capri Legging is a cute and versatile addition to the family. This version of a lucy classic capri legging has well-placed side seams that are slimming and will make you look good from behind. Perfect for all your studio classes.

  • Anti-chafe flat locked seams
  • Gusset inset for full range of movement and comfort
  • Moisture-wicking & compressive lucy powermax
  • Body hugging
  • Low-rise
