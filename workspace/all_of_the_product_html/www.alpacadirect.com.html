More Info

This alpaca roving comes from our alpacas on a ranch in Hayden Idaho. We hand shear each alpaca once per year and process the fiber locally into beautiful roving. Please note this is straight off the ranch so there may be some debris in the roving. A picture of the alpaca is available upon request with purchase.
