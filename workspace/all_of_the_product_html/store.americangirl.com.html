"Like most AG clothes, this is a well made, well-fitting dress. However, on my computer screen at least, it reads as more of a pink/mauve than it actually is. I understand that the coral/melon-y pink of it, especially the tulle, coordinates with the rest of Isabelle's collection, but I was hoping for something more straight up pink. And a lighter color.
She looks adorable in the dress, of course, but the color isn't what I'd hoped for. Also, the headpiece is an odd combination of something very beautiful and well made (the bejeweled metal comb) and something disappointing. The decorative part curls and flops over, with nothing to hold it up.
The bracelet and slippers are OK, though the slippers in the dance case fit better and are easier to get on."
