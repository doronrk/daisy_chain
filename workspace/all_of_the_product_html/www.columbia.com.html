Features

This waterproof, seam sealed and insulated winter boot is a true workhorse for activities in cold winter weather. Fortified with new hardware, including bombproof eyelets for easy and secure lacing, we’ve also added double rivets for durability. The reflective lining traps body heat to amplify the warming power of 200 grams of Omni-Heat® insulation. When terrain becomes truly treacherous and unpredictable, the dual compound high traction rubber keeps you safe and stable.


Construction:
  • Omni-Heat thermal reflective, insulated
  • Full waterproof seam sealed
  • Full length Techlite shell and midsole for lightweight comfort and flexibility
  • Full length Omni-Grip outsole with dual compound lug patterns
  • Waterproof leather for unsurpassed warmth
  • Articulated collar design lends freedom of movement without sacrificing protection
  • Features a waterproof system which is superior in the ability to keep the moisture away from the foot
  • 200 grams insulation rated -25F/-32C
  • Weather sensitive outsole for multiple hardness for greater traction in extreme cold conditions
  • Surprisingly lightweight
  • Weight: size 9, ½ pair = 24 oz/680.39g
  • Imported


Fabric:
  • UPPER
    Full waterproof seam sealed leather
  • INSULATION
    200 grams Omni-Heat technology
  • MIDSOLE
    Techlite lightweight technology
  • OMNI-GRIP OUTSOLE
    High traction rubber with dual-density for better traction over varied terrain

In my quest to find a cold weather boot, the Bugaboot Plus II Omni-Heat kept appearing in the top ten winter boot review sites as one of the best on the market. It boasts complete waterproofing, a patented heat-reflecting/moisture-wicking built-in lining, and a lightweight, yet supportive outer sole and upper that fit comfortably under pants.
I needed a pair of boots that can withstand sub-zero temperatures for long stretches outdoors (12+ hours, sometimes low activity). While I thought these might be a little light for this challenge, I couldn't believe the first time I put them on, they actually got warmer the longer I wore them. Really impressive heat retention capabilities.
I wear a 10.5 shoe (US), my feet tend to fit a bit wide in some brands. I purchased the 11 so I could double up socks if ever necessary, but they are roomy & great fit with just one pair of thick socks.
I think I will be a repeat customer if these ever wear out. Well-done, Columbia; the high accolades are well-deserved.
I would recommend this product.

How to Measure

How to Measure

  • Fold up at dotted line.
  • Place against a wall or other 90 degree surface.

    Place heel against fold/wall on flat level surface.
  • Mark the end of the longest toe with a pen or pencil.
  • Measure both feet and use the larger measurement.
  • Read measurement and compare to size chart.
  • When comparing on the size chart, determine the closest larger measurement and use that number to determine your shoe size for your country’s shoe measurement standard.
