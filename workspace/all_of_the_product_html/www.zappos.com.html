Brooks Adrenaline GTS 15

Video

Item Information

  • View the size chart
  • View the Video Description for this product! (Please note that the color shown in the video may no longer be available.)
  • Can't have meat without potatoes, and you can't have an exceptional pavement-pounding sesh without the Brooks® Adrenaline GTS 15!
  • Successor to the Adrenaline GTS 14, SKU# 8235365.
  • Built for the moderate overpronator who requires a combination of cushioning and stability.
  • Lightweight, breathable, moisture-managing Element mesh and synthetic uppers protect feet from the various elements.
  • Adjustable mid-foot saddle pulls from the heel up into the eyestays for added support.
  • Breathable, moisture-wicking Element linings keep feet cooler and drier.
  • Removable molded foam insole supplies light underfoot cushioning.
  • BioMoGo DNA is a blend of earth-friendly BioMoGo and responsive Brooks DNA, and has a gender-friendly cushioning design, making for a dynamic ride.
  • Progressive Diagonal Rollbar® (PDRB) is a tri-density midsole designed to promote progressive pronation control, as well as smoother transitions throughout the gait cycle.
  • Redesigned medial midsole is now higher, bringing more material, structure, and integrity to the heel counter, upper, and foot.
  • Domed midsole sidewalls prevent collapse for a truly supportive ride.
  • Lateral midsole pod compresses more easily to allow for optimal heel-to-toe fluidity.
  • Full-length, segmented Crash Pad ensures smooth transitions.
  • Blown rubber forefoot.
  • HPR Plus delivers long-lasting durability in high-wear areas of the outsole.
  • Flextra rubber outsole piece offers a varied degree of support corresponding with gender and weight for enhanced flexibility.
  • Omega Flex Grooves allow for more natural forefoot movement and fluidity.
  • Universal Sprung Platform enhances fit and function.
  • Imported.
  • Measurements:
    • Weight: 10 oz
  • Product measurements were taken using size 8.5, width B - Medium. Please note that measurements may vary by size.
