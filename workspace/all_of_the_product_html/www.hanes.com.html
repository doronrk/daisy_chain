    5. 5 out of 5 stars.
      2 years ago  

      Really Like this Bra

      I am 135 LBS, 5' 6" and wear a 36B usually. Since having children I have the all too common problem of not being able to fill my bra cups because of mass lost on the top of my breasts. This makes wearing a typical wire, demi-cup bra uncomfortable, unflattering and not supportive.I thought about buying those silicone inserts to fill the empty space, but I don't want to be carrying around all that this summer in the heat. SO, I thought I would try a simple wire-free comfotable bra. I ordered 6 different ones from Hanes and really like this one. I ordered a medium even though the chart put me in a small. It may prove to stretch and be too big, because I am on the tightest hooks in the back, but it fits well now. This bra comforms to my bustline and has extra coverage at the top, where I need it. The is a little space in the cups but that is easily solved by inserting those little foam inserts (like you find in swimsuits) into the bra. Underneath the cups there is a space you can shove these into the bra. The bra has nice stretchy foam cups that are flattering to my shape. It has nice support seams underneath where a wire would normally be. These seams are a little uncomfortable at first but I am already getting used to it, and once washed I think it will be fine. The back band could have been done a little better but it still lays fairly smooth. Overall I recommend this bra and plan on buying lots of them. There is room for improvement in the back band, and the fabric used on the under cup seaming if Hanes wanted to improve the bra, but I am happy with it the way it is. Glad there is one pretty color, but would be nice if there were a rainbow of them. Thanks Hanes!
