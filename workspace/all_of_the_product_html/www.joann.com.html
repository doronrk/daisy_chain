Description

Line your fabrics with the all-climate Roc-Lon Specialsuede Lining. This polyester blend fabric is great to use for your draperies and linens and can be used with printed fabrics to eliminate sun fading and transfer effect of the print. Apply a thin layer of insulating foam to the drapery fabric so that the non-porous lining offers maximum room insulation. Available in classic shades, this suede lining comes with a lustrous texture to add elegance to your living room or bedroom space. This water-repellent and stain- and UV-resistant lining is designed to last you for years.

  • Width: 54 inches
  • Content: 70% polyester and 30% cotton
  • Care: Dry cleanable fabric
  • Made in USA
  • Brand: Roc-Lon
  • Collection: Special Suede
  • Available in variety of colors and sizes, each sold separately
