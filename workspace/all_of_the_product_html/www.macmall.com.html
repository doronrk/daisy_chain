Overview
ERA is the smallest, lightest, most comfortable headset we've ever created. Advanced ear fittings feature a new design that uses the natural contour of the ear to create a secure fit so it's comfortable enough to wear all day.
  • MacMall Part #: 9832115 
  • Manufacturer Part #: JC01-02-US 
  • Platform: Universal 

DESIGNED FOR COMFORT

ERA is the smallest, lightest, most comfortable headset we've ever created. Advanced ear fittings feature a new design that uses the natural contour of the ear to create a secure fit so it's comfortable enough to wear all day.

BE HEARD ANYWHERE

Military-grade NoiseAssassin 4.0 eliminates background noise, so even if you're in a crowd, the only thing they'll hear on the other end is the sound of your voice.

STAY HANDS-FREE

Access your phone with your voice to stay hands-free. The Jawbone App lets you program the TALK button to easily access Siri on iPhone and Google Now on Android directly from the headset.

AUDIO:

  • Improved higher efficiency speaker
  • Enhanced EQ for natural sound
  • Advanced mic array for crystal-clear call quality
  • Voice Activity Sensor

POWER AND BATTERY:

  • On/Off Button
  • Up to 4 hours of talk time
  • Battery life displayed on compatible devices with the Jawbone App

CONTROLS:

  • Single multi-function button; answer/end/reject/swap
  • Last number redial, Voice Dial
  • Music Play/Pause
  • Battery Announcement

BLUETOOTH:

  • Wireless Range: at least 33 feet (10m)
  • Bluetooth Smart
  • Supports Enhanced Data Rate (EDR)
  • Profiles Supported: HFP 1.6, A2DP 1.2, AVRCP 1.4, HSP 1.2, SPP

CONNECTIONS:

  • Bluetooth
  • Micro-USB
  • Simultaneous Multipoint: connect with two devices at the same time
