Martian Passport Smartwatch Product Details

Martian Passport Smartwatch

Martian Passport Smartwatch
Merge class with technology when you use the Martian Passport SmartWatch - White/Silver/White. This SmartWatch is compatible with most of the latest Android and Apple devices. It connects to your device using its Bluetooth 4.0 technology. It allows you to make and answer calls, send texts, create events, and more using its noise cancelling microphone and directional speaker. Its OLED Display shows caller information, Text readouts and more at 96x16 pixels while the RGB LED light & Soft Touch Vibration notify you of your incoming calls and messages. The rechargeable Lithium-Polymer battery gives the watch a 7 day standby life and over 2 hours of talk time. It can be recharged in just two hours through the Micro USB port. It is made of a Stainless Steel Casing, Scratch resistant glass crystal, fire retardant resin back plate and white leather belt. It also has a Quartz analog watch with a battery that lasts up to 2 years. Buy the Martian Passport SmartWatch - White/Silver/White today!

The Perfect Hands-Free Partner to Your Smartphone
