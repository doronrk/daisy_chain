Whether it’s full of minty ice cream or a warming, spicy chai latte, our Peppermint bowl adds a fun, festive touch to any meal. Bowl features decorative candy cane striping and cleans up easily in the dishwasher.

  • Manufacturer: Sur La Table
  • Material: Earthenware
  • Care: Dishwasher safe
  • Use: Microwaveable, but may get hot. Use care when handling
  • Dimensions: 2¾" x 4¼"
  • Capacity: 8 oz.
  • Made in China exclusively for Sur La Table
