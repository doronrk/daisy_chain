Product Details

The BigOne

As the name indicates, the BigOne is huge. It's the size of a (very) small room and fits as many people as a car. But its greatness is about more than its size. It improves family relations, as you can all snuggle together without squirming and kicking, it tires the kids out as it doubles as a bouncy castle, and it even pleases grumpy teens when they realize that it impresses their friends. The super-soft filling is made of premium, shredded Durafoam. Unlike bead-filled bags, it absorbs your body weight evenly and doesn't compress over time, but stays airy and supple like a sit-able cloud.

What's Included:

  • The BigOne Khakisac
  • Eskimo Phur BigOne Cover
  • Premium BigOne Duffel Bag

About Eskimo Phur Covers

As intimated by the clever use of the "ph" instead of the traditional "f," no animals were harmed in the making of Lovesac Phurs they're fake, but they're real soft. Made of 100% polyester fibers, this phur is extremely stain resistant and really quite durable nearly anything will wash out of this phur, no matter the color. Its bonded woven fabric backing removes most stretch and stiffens the fabric adding loft and durability. This is considered a medium-pile phur long enough to run your fingers through, but short enough to avoid getting messy.

  • 100% synthetic faux fur
  • Cotton-poly backing
  • Short to medium pile
  • Machine-washable / hang dry

Eskimo Phur is a cream colored faux fur dream. Off-white undertones and a gleaming white sheen at the tips allow Eskimo Phur to work as white, or cream, depending on the dcor. Eskimo Phur has a unique rose-shaped bottle-cap-sized repeating pattern twisted into the nap of the phur. Eskimo Phur is bonded to a sturdy layer of poly-cotton backing giving it stiffness and loft beneath its ultimately soft exterior. Goes great with browns, blacks, blues, neutrals, and just about anything.

My Eskimo Phurball
I saw this product being featured at a website, which led me to research it some more and watch the YouTube Videos made by Lovesac. Shawny D. sold me on the sac, so I ended up at their Towson Mall store. Unfortunately all they had was the Supersac, so it was a bit difficult to visualize the size of the BigOne. I went with my gut instinct and blindly purchased the BigOne.
The naked sac arrived first and after fluffing it for a couple of days, I couldn't believe the sheer size of it. It fits perfectly on my bay window area in my man cave. However, with the size being so big, I've noticed that the seams were coming apart. I called the store and they immediately ordered me a warranty cover without any hassle. It came a day before the Eskimo Phur cover. The warranty cover was well constructed and I feel that it would definitely provide reinforcement support for this massive sac.
Now the Eskimo Phur... all I can say is wow!!! It's so luxuriously soft. The store did have a swatch of it, but it did not do justice at all to the real thing. Laying on the sac covered with the Eskimo Phur, all I keep doing is running my hands, fingers, legs along the material because it's so soft and comforting. I'm definitely glad I bought this cover. The price seems expensive, but what you get is quality and the craftsmanship of the construction is top notch.
This was a bit of an impulse buy, but I have no regrets. With the company's excellent warranty coverage, this piece of furniture will last me a lifetime.
