    Modernize your home decor with this Wakiaka Pagoda coffee table. This unique table features a translucent design and a rich coffee bean finish. The large 36-inch square design makes this coffee table an excellent choice for pairing with a sectional sofa or another L-shaped furniture configuration. The sturdy wood frame ensures durability, and the hidden inner storage with an access panel in the base is perfect for stashing books, magazines or throws out of sight.

    Made of veneer wood, this unique coffee table features a large countertop panel with plenty of room to display a lovely centerpiece or for guests to sit their drinks. This beautifully durable square coffee table adds a modern flair that looks great with almost any decor, and the shelf makes book or magazine storage simple.

    • Materials: Veneer wood
    • Finish: Coffee bean
    • Translucent design with pagoda-inspired leveled shelves
    • Supported by sturdy middle console with bottom panel support
    • Spacious counter top panel
    • Table dimensions: 36 inches wide x 36 inches deep x 16 inches high
