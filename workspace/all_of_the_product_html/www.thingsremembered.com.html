Toasting to friendship or hosting a more formal gathering where you're entertaining with flair, what says more about your good taste than an engraved decanter? -Formal gift? Engrave something classic, like "The Smith Family, Established 2011"
-Fun gift? Engrave a saying about their favorite libation: One of our favorites is "The last time I turned down whiskey, I didn't understand the question."
