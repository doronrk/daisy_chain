A Nesting Puzzle is typically made of wood where everything seems to fit in place, though it's your job to disassemble and re-assemble the whole thing. It's up to you to somehow untangle the strings to unlock the nesting wooden pieces from each other. It's not easy, and it will require the utmost patience, fine motor skills, concentration, and critical thinking to undo the puzzle. The Square is the easiest of the Nesting Puzzles, but it still provides quite the challenge!
