Date:February 18, 2014
I work outside servicing ATM machines. I needed a pair of boots that were going to be waterproof and keep my feet dry and warm in the winter. I am a female but have a hard time finding good work boots in womens shoes. When I put these on in the store they felt amazing on my feet it was love at first feel. The love quickly fizzled.
I have had these boots almost 3 months. I stopped wearing them about 3 weeks ago. My feet were constantly wet and cold. I have never stepped in a puddle over the rubber sole and the only thing that has gotten above that is snow. The smell that is coming from them is horrible just the smell of mildew and feet. I work long hours and it is hard for me to get to a store to return them. Its going to happen today. Im willing to give another pair a chance if they have them in stock still.
