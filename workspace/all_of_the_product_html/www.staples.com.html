PRODUCT DETAILS

Google Chromecast Streaming Media Player sends online video, music, and web content to your TV from your smartphone, tablet, or laptop.

Google Chromecast is the easiest way to enjoy online video, music, and anything from the web on your TV. Plug it into any HDTV and control it with your existing smartphone, tablet, or laptop. No remotes required. Cast your favorites from Netflix, YouTube, HBO GO, Hulu Plus, Pandora, and Google Play Movies and Music, to your TV with the press of a button.

  • Stream HD videos, music, and anything on the web to your TV using your smartphone, tablet, or laptop
  • Supports Netflix, YouTube, HBO GO, Hulu Plus, Pandora, Google Play Movies and Music, ESPN, MLS, Crunchyroll and Google Photos, as well as anything on the web using Chrome browser
  • Works with Android, iOS®, and Chrome for Mac® and Windows®
  • Easy setup: Plug into any HDMI port on your TV and connect to your home WiFi network

This appears to be a high quality device. It was easy to set up, although making it work with Windows 7 was a bit of a challenge (I needed to disable a VPN that I didn't know I even had).

My main disappointment is that the capabilities are quite limited. I was able to display my browser window on my TV with ease. But the only streaming video service I use is Amazon Prime. While I could display that remotely in a window, I was unable to display it full screen. I am optimistic that Google will update the capabilities, but would not recommend this product to a friend until then.
One other issue, the device apparently interferes with our over-the-air antenna. We have to power down the device in order to view local TV.
