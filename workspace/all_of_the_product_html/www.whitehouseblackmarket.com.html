Available in Regular and Petite
Style in sleek structure when you layer up this season in ultra refined ponté knit— notch collar jacket with diamond stitching accents at the tops of each shoulder below military style epaulettes and gleaming goldtone buttons. Grosgrain trim follows the princess seams down the front and back for ultra chic touches.
  • Princess seamed stretch fit.
  • Notch collar. Hidden button front with two goldone button decorations and ruffled detail.
  • Slightly padded shoulders with diamond stitching and epaulettes with goldtone buttons.
  • Long sleeves with goldtone button closures and diamond stitching.
  • Inverted pleat on back peplum with diamond stitching and double goldtone button accents.
  • 70% viscose, 25% nylon, 5% elastane. Machine wash, cold; inside out. Imported.
  • Regular: Approximately 24” in length from shoulder.
  • Petite: Approximately 22.5” in length from shoulder.
