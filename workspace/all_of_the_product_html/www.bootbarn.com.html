    This vintage style distressed leather belt is a simple belt that can go with any pair of jeans. The buckle that comes on this belt is an antiqued, floral detailed round buckle with a rope edge. Centered on the buckle is a vintage style cross that features amber colored crystals that add a great look to this simple style. The buckle and keeper are removable, allowing you to personalize this belt with your own buckle!
