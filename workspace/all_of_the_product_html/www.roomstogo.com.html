Sleek and chic, the Castilla living room makes comfort look good! Decadently supple top grain leather in striking black covers everywhere the body touches for a luxurious lounging experience. Featuring plush pillowtop arms and contrasting stitching, each piece is perched on chrome finished legs for a stylishly modern aesthetic.
  • Upholstery: Leather wherever the body touches; all else blended leather
  • Cleaning Instructions: Proper cleaning and maintenance is suggested by taking advantage of Rooms To Go's force field leather protection. Available in all showrooms and selected areas online.

Featuring luxuriously supple black top grain leather covering wherever the body touches, this chic sofa is further accented by contrasting stitching detail. Plush pillowtop arms and striking chrome finished legs represent the careful balance of practicality and style.
