Our exclusive StayShape styles fit you perfectly, and always keep

Microtherm™
MicroTherm™ is First Ascent's lightest, warmest down construction, which pairs high quality 800 fill with some of the narrowest channel baffling in the outdoor industry.
Flexion
Flexible, breathable, and adaptable, our proprietary Flexion stretch‑woven constructions deliver dimensional stretch for all‑day comfort and offer an extremely versatile, high‑performance alternative to traditional fabrics.
FreeCool™
Our proprietary FreeCool fabrics stay cool and breathable in the warmest conditions, with lightweight yarn blends and unique textile construction.
FreeCool™ Pro
Our proprietary FreeCool Pro fabrics fuse active xylitol‑treated fibers with a thermally protective finish to create a cooling experience during humid weather or heated activity. Xylitol, a naturally occurring compound, creates a cooling skin‑sensation by reacting with moisture to reduce perceived temperatures. A thermally protective finish on the exterior also prevents the overheating effects of the sun's infrared radiation. Both elements remain 80% effective after 20 washes.
FreeDry™
Our proprietary FreeDry fabrics use cutting‑edge performance—featuring natural and synthetic fibers—to deliver the most efficient combination of wicking moisture away from the body to the garment surface, and rapid drying to maintain optimal comfort while active.
FreeDry™ Cotton
FreeDry is Eddie Bauer's new exclusive line of moisture management fabrics. The natural example—FreeDry Cotton—utilizes microfiber technology that dissipates moisture and dries faster than traditional cotton. The multi‑component construction wicks moisture rapidly from your skin during heated activity to facilitate cooling, with yarn‑specific properties that never wash out. It's the comfort of cotton with the performance of a synthetic.
FreeShade™
Our proprietary FreeShade fabrics utilize textile construction to deliver high‑degree solar protection from intense summer rays. Blocking sun with a UPF rating ranging from 15 to 50+, FreeShade prevents the burn by acting as a material barrier to harmful rays.
