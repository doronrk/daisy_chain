Excellent for beer can chicken

(5 of 5 people found this Amazon.com review helpful)

By B. Lasota

Comments about Mr. Bar-B-Q, Inc. Beer Can Chicken Roaster-0, 0-2.402" x 6.299" x 6.299":

I've used this product once and it's very easy to set the beer can and chicken on the grill. The only problem is getting everything off the grill once it's scorching hot. The way I get the chicken off is to get a flat cookie sheet, slide the chicken standing up with the holder onto the sheet, and take it into the kitchen. Some would say just lift the chicken off while it's still on the grill, but it tastes better if you leave it on there after pulling from the grill for a little while. I like to eat it while it's hot, so that's why it comes off right away. You could also leave it on the grill after shutting the grill off to cool down, but then the chicken won't be piping hot.

If you like full chickens to be cooked on the grill, then shove a beer can up in it with this can holder! My cheap neighbors have been doing it for years but without something like this. But then they wonder why the chicken falls over on occasion and the one side gets burned.
