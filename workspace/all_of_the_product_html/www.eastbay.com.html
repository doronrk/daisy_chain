Details

Weider Power Tower Get awesome fitness results with the Weider Power Tower, which includes a variety of upper-body and core workout capabilities. Push-up station encourages proper form to build and tone your chest muscles and triceps. Multi-grip station helps refine and define back and arm muscles. Like the push-up station, the dip station works your triceps and chest, but in a differing variation. Vertical knee-raise station tones and sculpts your midsection, helping you trim inches around your waist. Made of durable steel tubing, the Weider Power Tower promises long-lasting durability to help you achieve your long-term fitness goals. Sz: 57" x 41" x 84".

Please note: There is a flat rate shipping charge of $160.00 for this item. This item is shipped directly from the manufacturer and can only be shipped to the 48 Contiguous States. Expedited shipping and gift boxing options are not available. The item must be shipped to a street address only. The freight company will contact the customer for delivery times and instructions. Upon delivery, the freight company will place item in the room closest to the front door of the home.
