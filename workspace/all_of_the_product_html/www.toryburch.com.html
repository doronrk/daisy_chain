DESCRIPTION

Color:
How to take a little black dress from simple to special? Add a waist-defining peplum. Our Ashley Dress does just that, creating an elegantly tailored hourglass silhouette in a refined crepe fabric. Sheer organza cap sleeves, a split-front neckline and velvet binding on the bodice finish this timeless shape with understated polish — a chic office-to-evening-out style.
STYLED WITH:

Style Tips

This classic dress is chic on its own — and is also a great blank canvas for accessorizing. Add a jeweled necklace, and wear your hair up, to play up the sheer sleeves and slit neckline. A patterned clutch is a graphic finishing touch.

DETAILS & FIT

  • Heavy crepe.
  • Fitted.
  • V-neck.
  • Short sleeves; sleeve length: 11.25" (28.2 cm)
  • Invisible zipper with hook-and-eye closure at center back.
  • 38" (95.4 cm) in length; hits above knee.

FABRIC & CARE

  • 98% polyester, 2% spandex.
  • Dry-clean only.
