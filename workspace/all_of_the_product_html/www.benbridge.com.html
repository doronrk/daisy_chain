    Couture Collection Diamond Ring 18K


    The Couture Collection by Ben Bridge features hand-selected diamonds of superior quality mounted in Platinum or 18K gold. Each piece is one of a kind, certified by the Gemological Institute of America or the American Gem Society, and held to the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

    Diamond Details

    • Cut: Radiant
    • Carat Weight: 1.27 carat
    • Color Grade: Fancy Yellow


    Product Details
    • Additional Stones: 130 round brilliant near-colorless and yellow diamonds equaling 1 carat total weight, I1 minimum
    • Metal Type: 18K white, yellow and rose gold
    • Mounting Style: Double halo with bead-set detailing


    The Couture Collection: Exquisite rarity, refined elegance. Exclusively available online and in select stores. Contact Customer Service at (888) 448-1912 or info@benbridge.com for more information.
