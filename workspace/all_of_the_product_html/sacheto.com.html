Bosch Tools Basic Power Box Jobsite Radio, Model PB360S

The Bosch PB360S Power Box Jobsite Radio brings a premium listening experience to the workplace in a nearly indestructible package. Weatherproof and shockproof, the Power Box can withstand a summer storm or a 10 foot drop onto solid concrete and survive to pump out your favorite jobsite jam. A speaker on each corner and down-firing subwoofer distribute 360 degrees of sound at volume levels powerful enough to overcome the usual workplace raucous.

Inputs galore ensure theres always something good playing. If you cant find a good radio station with the digital tuner, the AUX In Port allows connection of an iPod or MP3 player, and the Powered USB Port and SD Card Slot can play songs directly from flash media. Never worry about finding an outlet, because the PB360S can run on the 14.4V or 18V FatPack and SlimPack lithium-ion batteries that power all of your Bosch Cordless Tools. And when plugged in, the Bosch Power Box also doubles as a charger for those batteries.
