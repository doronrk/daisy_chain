    RadioShack Cash Promotion FAQs

    • How does the Fall ’14 Bounceback coupon work?
      • Any customer who makes a purchase from 10/10/14 – 12/31/14 will receive a coupon worth $10 off a $40 or more purchase. Coupons are valid for 21 days, exact dates will be shown on the coupon.
    • Where can I earn the Fall ’14 Bounceback coupons?
      • At any participating RadioShack locations, and on RadioShack.com.
      • In participating stores, the coupon will print on the original receipt. For online purchases, coupon will be sent via email.
    • Where can I redeem the coupon I received for my in-store purchase?
      • Coupons may be redeemed at any participating RadioShack location. One $10 off $40 coupon may be redeemed per day on a minimum qualifying purchase of $40. Some product exclusions apply. Redeem by date will be specified on coupon.
    • Can I redeem the coupon I received for my in-store purchase coupon online?
      • Unfortunately, coupons earned for in-store purchases are not available for redemption on RadioShack.com. Visit RadioShack.com/locations to find a local store.
    • Where can I redeem the coupon I received for my online purchase?
      • Coupons earned for online purchases will be redeemed online on RadioShack.com.
    • Can I redeem my coupon with other offers/discounts?
      • Only one coupon may be redeemed per day. It cannot be combined with any other coupons or certain other offers, including price match and employee discount. Coupon is valid on the purchase of sale items.
    • How long do I have to redeem my coupon?
      • Redemption period is 21 days from issue of coupon. The specific redeem by date will be indicated on the coupon.
    • When will I receive my coupon?
      • In participating stores, the coupon will print on the original receipt. For online purchases, coupon will be sent via email.
    • I accidentally lost my receipt with the coupon, can I get a replacement?
      • Unfortunately, coupons are not replaceable.
    • How many coupons can I redeem at once?
      • Only one coupon may be redeemed per day. A minimum qualifying purchase of $40 is required. Each coupon has a unique code and is valid for one use only.
    • Do I have to have my original receipt to redeem my coupon?
      • No, but you need to present the actual coupon for redemption. Each coupon has a unique code and is valid for one use only. Multiple printouts or photocopies will not be accepted.
    • Can I redeem my coupon on gift card purchases?
      • Gift cards and entertainment cards are not eligible for purchase with the coupons. However, you can earn coupons by purchasing gift cards and entertainment cards.
    • What purchases can I use my coupon on?
      • Coupons are valid on all purchases of $40 or more except Apple, Beats by Dr. Dre, Fitbit and Quirky products, gaming hardware/software, airtime, gift/entertainment cards, services, special orders, refurbished items. Coupons cannot be redeemed in-store or online on these dates: 11/27–11/29/14, or online 12/1/14.
    • If I return an item, is my coupon still valid?
      • Returning any items which result in a non-qualifying purchase amount may invalidate the coupon.
    • If I purchase a gift that qualifies for the coupon and the recipient returns it, is my coupon still redeemable?
      • Returning any items which result in a non-qualifying purchase amount may invalidate the coupon.
