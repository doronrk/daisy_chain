Product Details

It's never too early to learn how to ride! If your little one has mastered walking, then he or she is ready to get cruisin' on this 12 Classic™ no-pedal balance bike from Strider®. Perfect for children from 18 months to 5 years old, this stable and steady bike boasts a mini saddle for proper riding posture and frame-integrated footrests to promote balance; the handlebar and saddle height are adjustable to accommodate growing kids.


Features

  • Skill-building, entry-level 12" no-pedal bike
  • Saddle and handlebars are adjustable
  • Durable steel frame
  • Standard 22 mm steel handlebar with soft urethane grips
  • Plastic, all-weather mini saddle designed especially for small hips
  • One-piece wheels feature a raised-letter 5-spoke design with 10 bearing support gussets to keep them light and strong
  • EVA polymer tires are puncture proof
  • Premium cartridge wheel bearings for smooth rolling; never need to be adjusted
  • Launchpad footrest with grip tape
  • Rear brake mount lets you add a foot-operated brake later (brake sold separately)
  • Assembles easily with the included wrench
  • Age: 18 months to 5 years
  • Max Rider Weight: 60 lbs
  • Weight: 6.7 lbs
  • Manufacturer's 2-year warranty
  • Made in China
