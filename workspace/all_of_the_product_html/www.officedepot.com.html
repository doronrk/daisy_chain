Description

Work and play in style with this new sleek, sturdy tablet
  • A bright, luminous 8.9" HD display makes it large enough to get things done, yet small enough to take with you everywhere. 
  • Installed with Android 5.0 Lollipop, the latest operating system after Android KitKat. 
  • Immerse yourself in great,distortion-free sound with front-facing HTC BoomSound speakers. 
  • Built-in webcam and microphone for conferencing, video and voice messaging. 
  • Dual cameras (8-megapixel back and 1.6-megapixel front) are available for taking photos, videos and more. 
  • Powered by state-of-the-art nVidia Tegra K1 processor, with a speed of 2.3GHz. 
  • Made for Google Play choose from more than 600,000 apps and 4 million books. 
  • Wi-Fi (802.11ac) and Bluetooth 4.0 make it easy to live life on the go. 
  • Takes just seconds to switch between editing documents, browsing the Internet, watching movies or listening to your music. 
  • The micro-USB port offers the flexibility you need. 
  • Stays powered for up to 9 hours.  Battery life will vary depending on the product configuration, product model, applications loaded on the product, power management setting of the product, and the product features used by the customer. As with all batteries, the maximum capacity of this battery will decrease with time and usage.
  • Sound is more immersive, layered and distortion-free with front-facing HTC BoomSound speakers. 
  • 2048 x 1536 pixel display (QXGA). 
  • Weighs just under 15 oz.
