Description

Get on your bike and ride.

Upright Cyclist designed the Men's 12.5 Oz Riding Denim Pant for the serious commuters out there. This pant's American Cone Denim is the type of fabric that looks better with age, and the double gusseted crotch ensures durability as well as uninhibited pedaling. Its modern fit stays loose around the waist, but gradually tapers toward the cuff in order to stay out of your drivetrain. A higher waist makes sure outgoing traffic doesn't see your backside. Upright Cyclist added reflective panels inside the cuffs to help keep you visible in low light.

  • American Cone Denim
  • Modern fit, tapered legs
  • Higher waist
  • Double gusseted crotch
  • Reflective panels inside cuffs
