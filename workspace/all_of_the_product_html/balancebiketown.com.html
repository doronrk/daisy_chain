You won't find too many balance bikes designed for teens and adults which is why the 20" Strider Sport was created. Designed for riders weighing up to 242 pounds. This bike is a great option for adults who want to learn how to ride a bike, or teens and adults with special needs who can't ride a traditional bike with pedals. 

Weighs 21.2 Lbs

Adjustable handle bar allows teens and adults of various height to use this bike.

Seat height ranges from 26.5 - 32.25 inches

Brakes on both front and rear wheels.

Ergonomically designed hand levers.  

Threadless steering bearings allow for a smooth ride. 

Removable footrest allows for advanced riding.

All surface tires with 36-spoke alloy rims and hubs. 

Comes with a two year manufactures warranty and free shipping.
