    My new Fave!
    Date:September 18, 2012
    I had recently been using the Rainforest Moisture Hair Butter as my daily conditioner (I still love it and use it twice a week), but I have so much hair on my head that this was getting a bit pricey. I decided to try this Banana conditioner since the cost per ounce ratio is better and it has such rave reviews. I'm usually weary of trying conditioner that is described as being for "normal" or "all hair types" because I have a whole lot of wavy crazy hair on my head that doesn't typically fit that moisture profile. I've been using the Banana conditioner for two weeks and I love it! It's not super thick like the hair butter so I actually find it easier to spread through the many layers of my hair (without it being watery - a definite no-no for conditioners in my book) , and I've noticed an improvement in the condition of my hair (super soft and decreased frizziness). The scent is nice and soft, subtle banana and other fruit/floral notes that aren't overwhelming. I'm hooked!
