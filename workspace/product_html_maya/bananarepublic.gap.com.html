      Itchy wool, not proportioned properly for a petite

      I really, really wanted to like this coat. I've been looking for an everyday winter coat that isn't a down-filled puffer. I thought this was it. But I found a number of things wrong with it. The wool is itchy and a bit stiff. When the coat is buttoned up, the lapels won't lie flat (you can see it in the photo). Look closely at the collar and you'll see a metal hook & eye meant to keep the collar closed when the coat is buttoned up all the way (which I wouldn't do, because the wool is too itchy). I would cut these off, because they detract from the stylishness of the coat. And what's up with the belt? It was long enough to wrap around my waist twice. And last, but not least, I'm very petite (4'11", 90lbs) and thought a 00P would fit. It didn't. The shoulders were a tad wide, the torso and waist were also too wide, the bust placement was too low. One side of the coat flared out in an odd way, like it was stitched wrong, and I couldn't get it to lie flat. Sadly, I'm returning it.
