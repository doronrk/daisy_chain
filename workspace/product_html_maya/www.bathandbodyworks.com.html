    "When I first smelled this in the store it smelled fine and I really didn't think it was overly strong. I was so excited to use it in my kitchen cause I usually go for citrus scents for use in my kitchen but the scent was just unbearable to me and I found out later everyone in my house had been avoiding using this soap because of its noxious scent. I suffer from migraines and a scent this strong could definitely trigger a migraine for me so I felt I had no choice but to return this to B&BW and exchange it for a scent that I know is "safe" for my medical condition. The bottle is beautiful, just gorgeous actually and looks beautiful at whatever sink you choose to place it by. I'm also loving the fact that B&BW has improved their pumps, it seems the pumps on all their foaming hand soaps I have purchased no longer get stuck. I'm also loving the beautiful pearlized pumps that some of the hand soaps have on them this winter season and I think this hand soap has the beautiful pearlized pump which goes beautifully with the winterscape scene on the bottle."
