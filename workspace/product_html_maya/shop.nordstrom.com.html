    Details & Care

    Flat-front crepe marocain pants with darting at the back waist feature a modern, cropped length.

    • 28 1/2" inseam; 15" leg opening; 11" front rise; 16" back rise (size 12).
    • Zip fly with hook-and-bar closure and interior anchor button.
    • Front slant pockets.
    • 63% triacetate, 37% polyester; dry clean.
    • Made in the USA.
    • By St. John Collection.
