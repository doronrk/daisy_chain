PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:

Malas are Tibetan rosaries, traditionally used for special Buddhist practice. Because Tibetan Buddhism suggests that there are 108 negative emotions to overcome, malas such as this Rudraksha Mala are strung with 108 beads, one bead for each mantra.

    Features

  • Hand-strung beads.
  • Rudarksha seed on durable nylon coated wire.
  • Finished with a cotton tassel.
  • Use for meditation or spiritual practice.
  • Handmade by fair trade artisans.

    Details

  • Designed in: Nepal
  • Material(s): Rudraksha seed, nylon coated wire
  • Dimensions: 7-9 mm beads.

  • Keywords: japa, japa mala, malas, rosary, Rudraksha, Rudraksha seed, meditation, fair trade, handmade
